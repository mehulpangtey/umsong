Highlands of Meghalaya

Height altitude and foggy environment are the factor which enriches the flavour and the potency of the tea is influenced by the organic farming which is still practice in this region. What's sets apart the organic farming done here is the quality of the soil and fertility which is nourished by rarest species 'blue worm' , which is unique in this region of the world.
